Comment Policy


Good Rules to Follow

- If requsting a feature, check to make sure it was not already requested! If it is, you may want to upvote/like the comment it!

- If reporting a bug, check to make sure it was also not already requested! If it is, you may want to upvote/like the commment

Things to Keep in Mind

- DO NOT WRITE ANY HATE OF ANY KIND

- DO NOT DISCRIMINATE

- DO NOT SPAM

- If your comment is flagged more than 5 times, it will be sent for review by one of our moderators.

- Please use English

Possible Punishments

Hate & Spam Punishments

First Time: mute for 5-20 minutes, however, you may only get a warning

Second Time: mute for 45-120 (2 hours) minutes

Third Time: Temp ban for 12-48 (2 days) hours

Fourth Time: ALL comments are held for review for 1-4 (1 month) weeks

Fifth Time: Temp ban for 6-48 (4 years) months, however, if it is very severe, you may get a 5 or more years, or a permanent ban

Discrimination Punishments

First Time, ALL comments held for review for 1-4 (1 month) weeks.

Second Time: Temp ban for 12-48 (2 days) hours

Third Time: Temp ban for 6-48+ (4 years) months

Fourth Time: Permanent ban
